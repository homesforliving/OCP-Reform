Where is it 'suitable' to build an apartment?

Criteria: all properties that are:

  • Within 400m of a bus stop, and
  • Within 400m of ammenities such as grocery stories, banks, schools, etc.

This isn't meant as a comprehensive survey of Victoria ammenities, but rather an illustration of which neighbourhoods are well-resourced and which ones aren't.

See the underlying data and code on Github.
